                    Collabnet Connector Framework - Samples
				    ---------------------------------------

About:
   The CCF Samples are provided to give the users a kick-start on using the CCF framework. You will
   have to follow the instructions given below to get CCF working on your environment.

Instructions:
   1. Set the JAVA_HOME environment variable in the bin/setEnv.bat
   2. Choose the wiring you want to implement from the sub-directories available,
   and follow the instructions in the README.txt available there. 

Happy Connecting!
