Improving the system processes:

1. In XP, disable the Themes service and remove the unwanted programs.

2. Uncheck the two radio buttons and apply to all the subfolders in the properties of all drives.

3. Do a DiskCleanUp and DiskFragmentation for all the drives.

4. Configure the msconfig properly.
